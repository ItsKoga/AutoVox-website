Privacy Policy

AutoVox may collect the following types of information:

- User Information: This includes your Discord username, user ID, and server ID when you interact with the Bot.

- To provide Bot functionalities such as automatic channel creation and thread management.

- To improve and enhance the features of the Bot based on user feedback and data.

4. Data Sharing

- We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties.

- We may share limited information with trusted partners who help us operate and improve the Bot, but only to the extent necessary.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

- Request access to the information we store about you.

- Request deletion of your data, where applicable.

- Request corrections to any inaccurate information.

To exercise these rights, please contact the Bot developers or server administrators.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, the “Effective Date” will be updated accordingly. Continued use of the Bot constitutes acceptance of these changes.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the server support channel or by direct message to the Bot’s developers.
